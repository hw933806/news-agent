## Scope decision (locked)
Include **read-through news** — competitors, partners, suppliers, and customers — not just
the 5 names themselves. Competitor/partner moves are often the highest signal. Also include
**macro signals** (below), ranked by relevance to the names they actually move.

## Cross-cutting sources (apply to all)
- SEC EDGAR full-text + 8-K alerts; each company's IR / earnings calendar
- Analyst rating & price-target changes (initiations, up/downgrades)
- Insider transactions (Form 4)

## Low-signal — explicitly de-prioritize / exclude
- Retail-investor forums (Reddit, StockTwits), pure price-action headlines, listicles,
  syndicated PR with no new info, "X stock is up/down today" wire pieces.
