# Ferguson to acquire FloWorks for ~$1.6B (announced 2026-07-13)

Ferguson entered a definitive agreement to acquire FloWorks (FWI Holdings, Inc.),
a leading industrial distributor of valves and flow-control solutions, in an
all-cash transaction valued at ~$1.6 billion.

- **Strategic rationale:** expands Ferguson's non-residential / industrial
  value-added capabilities and lifts its stated total addressable market to
  ~$400 billion. Diversifies away from rate-sensitive residential RMI.
- **Synergies:** ~$45 million expected.
- **Balance sheet:** Ferguson expects to remain within its targeted net
  debt / adjusted EBITDA range of 1–2x upon closing.
- **Timing:** expected to close Q3 2026, subject to customary conditions and
  regulatory approvals.

Read-through: largest bolt-on/platform deal in the recent record; a deliberate
tilt toward industrial flow control as residential construction stays soft
(cf. Goldman's 2026-07-06 Buy→Neutral downgrade citing lackluster near-term
residential growth). Watch for closing confirmation and integration/synergy
updates on the next earnings call.
