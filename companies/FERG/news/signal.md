# FERG — Signal rules

Defines what counts as worth-an-email for Ferguson. The ranker uses these to score and
order items highest → lowest within the FERG block. Tune freely; this is per-stock.

## HIGH signal (lead the section)
- Earnings, guidance changes, pre-announcements, segment/margin commentary
- M&A (Ferguson as acquirer or target), large bolt-on acquisitions
- Capital allocation: buybacks, dividends, big capex, debt actions
- Management changes (CEO/CFO), board/activist involvement
- Analyst rating changes & material price-target moves
- Regulatory/legal actions of size
- Direct competitor earnings/guidance that reset the read on the category (WSO, CNM)
- Macro that directly hits the thesis: mortgage rates, housing starts, remodeling (LIRA)

## MEDIUM signal (include if room)
- Supplier price actions or capacity changes (Lennox, Carrier, Trane)
- Homebuilder demand commentary (LEN, DHI)
- New branch openings / distribution footprint expansion, large contract wins
- Industry trends in the trade press with read-through to Ferguson volumes/pricing

## LOW / EXCLUDE (do not email)
- Daily price-action headlines ("FERG up/down X%")
- Syndicated PR with no new info, listicles, "best stocks to buy" content
- Retail-forum chatter (Reddit/StockTwits)
- Minor personnel moves, routine marketing, sponsorships
