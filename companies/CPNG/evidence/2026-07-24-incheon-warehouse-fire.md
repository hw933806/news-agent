# Coupang Incheon fulfillment-center fire (Seoknam-dong)

A fire broke out at ~06:54 KST on Saturday 7/18 on the 6th floor of the 8-story
Seoknam-dong (Incheon) fulfillment center — one of Coupang's largest hubs (~299,000 m²),
serving Incheon, western Seoul, and northwestern Gyeonggi. It spread to the 7th floor and
was only brought under control ~61 hours later (~8pm Mon 7/20) — likely the longest
domestic logistics-center fire on record. All 121 people inside evacuated safely; only
minor injuries among firefighters. Combustible stored goods (household products, paper,
vinyl packaging) and the building's complex structure hampered firefighting. A separate,
minor fire at another Incheon Coupang center was contained with an extinguisher.

## Impact (as quantified 7/22–7/23)
- Damage estimates ~₩340B ($230M+); some estimates $270–450M for rebuild alone
  (excluding destroyed inventory and business interruption).
- Analysts caution that, with fire-related losses, Coupang's FY26 net result could swing
  to a loss exceeding last year's profit.
- Delivery disruption across Incheon / western Seoul during and after the fire.

## Company response & investigation
- Coupang Fulfillment Services will preemptively and fully compensate Rocket Growth (3P)
  sellers for inventory losses, ahead of insurance settlement and the fire probe.
  Final seller-compensation standards depend on the damage investigation; disputes could
  run months to >1 year.
- Joint police / fire / National Forensic Service investigation underway: examining the
  facility's automated-system records and electrical wiring, and whether sprinklers and
  fire alarms functioned. Cause not yet confirmed.

## Why it matters
Largest CPNG operational shock since the 2025 data breach: a direct hit to capacity in the
Seoul metro, a potential FY26 P&L swing, near-term delivery/service degradation (share risk
to Naver / Gmarket-AliExpress), and fire-safety-systems scrutiny that could invite
regulatory follow-up. Watch the 8/4 Q2 print for management's damage/insurance framing.
