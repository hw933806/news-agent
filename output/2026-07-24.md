# Daily Stock News — 2026-07-24

## CPNG — Coupang
- **Coupang** — **Major fire destroyed one of Coupang's largest fulfillment centers** (Seoknam-dong, Incheon; ~299,000 m², serving Incheon/western Seoul/NW Gyeonggi). The blaze broke out Sat 7/18 and was only contained after ~61 hours (~8pm Mon 7/20) — likely the longest logistics-center fire on record; all 121 workers evacuated safely. In-window developments now firming the impact: **damage estimates ~₩340B ($230M+)**, with some pegging rebuild-only cost at **$270–450M** (before inventory); analysts warn **FY26 could swing to a net loss exceeding last year's profit**. Coupang Fulfillment Services said it will **preemptively and fully compensate Rocket Growth (3P) sellers** for inventory losses ahead of insurance/investigation, and a **joint police / fire / National Forensic Service investigation** has begun — examining automated-system records, wiring, and whether sprinklers and alarms functioned. Deliveries in Incheon/western Seoul were disrupted (competitive-share risk vs Naver et al.). Q2 results land 8/4. ([Korea JoongAng Daily](https://www.koreajoongangdaily.com/korea/coupang-logistics-center-fire-contained-after-61-hours-authorities-prepare-for-investigation/12782747), [Business Insurance](https://www.businessinsurance.com/coupang-logistics-fire-losses-could-top-230m/))
