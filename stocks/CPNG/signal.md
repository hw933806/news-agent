# CPNG — Signal rules

Defines what counts as worth-an-email for Coupang. The ranker uses these to score and
order items highest → lowest within the CPNG block. Tune freely; this is per-stock.

## HIGH signal (lead the section)
- Earnings, guidance changes, segment margin commentary (Product Commerce vs
  Developing Offerings; progress toward 10%+ adj-EBITDA target)
- Regulatory actions: KFTC rulings/fines and the appeal of the KRW 162.8B search-ranking
  fine; the criminal probe; PIPC data-breach penalties; platform-regulation bills passing
- Data-breach aftermath: new liabilities, lawsuits, membership/WOW churn data
- Capital allocation: buybacks (the ~KRW 2T program), dividends, major capex
- Bank of Korea rate decisions; large KRW/USD moves (translation headwind)
- Competitive resets: Grand Opus (Gmarket+AliExpress JV) moves, Temu/AliExpress Korea
  share shifts, Naver Plus Store momentum, an Uber–Baemin deal closing
- M&A (Coupang as acquirer or target), Farfetch disposal/restructuring news
- Management changes (Bom Kim, CFO, Korea-unit CEO), board/activist involvement
- Analyst rating changes & material price-target moves
- Taiwan expansion milestones (new fulfillment centers, profitability commentary)

## MEDIUM signal (include if room)
- Coupang Eats vs Baemin share data; Coupang Play content/subscriber news
- FLC (3P fulfillment) and ads/cloud (Intelligent Cloud) progress — margin drivers
- Korea retail-sales / consumer data; tariff or cross-border e-commerce policy
- Global comp read-throughs (Amazon, MELI, Sea) only if they reset e-commerce multiples

## LOW / EXCLUDE (do not email)
- Daily price-action headlines ("CPNG up/down X%")
- Syndicated PR with no new info, listicles, "best stocks to buy" content
- Retail-forum chatter (Reddit/StockTwits)
- Routine product/marketing announcements, K-content licensing minutiae
