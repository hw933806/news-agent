# WRBY — Signal rules

Defines what counts as worth-an-email for Warby Parker. The ranker uses these to score
and order items highest → lowest within the WRBY block. Tune freely; this is per-stock.

## HIGH signal (lead the section)
- Earnings, guidance changes, comp/store-growth or margin commentary
- Google / Android XR glasses news: launch dates, pricing, delays, demand signals,
  changes to the $150M commitment or milestone equity
- Target shop-in-shop expansion (new location counts, economics)
- M&A (Warby as acquirer or target), strategic investments
- Management changes (co-CEOs/CFO), board/activist involvement
- Analyst rating changes & material price-target moves
- Tariff/trade-policy changes hitting eyewear imports (frames/lenses)
- Category resets: EssilorLuxottica or National Vision earnings/guidance;
  Meta AI-glasses momentum (unit numbers, new models, price cuts)

## MEDIUM signal (include if room)
- Managed-vision-care progress (Versant utilization, new insurance networks)
- Store-opening cadence vs the ~50/yr plan; retail traffic data
- Competitor smart-glasses launches or price moves (Meta $299 line, Ray-Ban Display intl rollout)
- Consumer-spending data with a discretionary-eyewear read-through

## LOW / EXCLUDE (do not email)
- Daily price-action headlines ("WRBY up/down X%")
- Syndicated PR with no new info, listicles, "best stocks to buy" content
- Retail-forum chatter (Reddit/StockTwits)
- Minor personnel moves, routine marketing, collab/drop announcements without financial substance
