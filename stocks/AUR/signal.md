# AUR — Signal rules

Defines what counts as worth-an-email for Aurora. The ranker uses these to score and
order items highest → lowest within the AUR block. Tune freely; this is per-stock.

## HIGH signal (lead the section)
- ANY safety incident involving a driverless truck (Aurora's or a competitor's) —
  thesis-critical even if minor
- Driverless network expansion: new lanes/routes, truck-count milestones
  (vs the 200+ trucks by end-2026 target), new markets beyond TX/NM/AZ
- Earnings, guidance, cash runway, capital raises / dilution (pre-revenue economics)
- OEM production milestones or slips: Volvo VNL Autonomous, International/Traton
  (Q2 2026 deployment), Continental mass production (2027), 2nd-gen hardware kit
- Major customer commitments (Hirschbach 500-truck intent, new driverless customers)
- Regulatory: FMCSA exemption rulings, NHTSA actions, state AV law changes
- Competitor resets: Kodiak (KDK) milestones/earnings, a competitor driverless launch
- Management changes (CEO/CFO), board/activist involvement
- Analyst rating changes & material price-target moves

## MEDIUM signal (include if room)
- Freight-market indicators (Cass, DAT spot rates, tonnage) — the demand backdrop
- Partner carrier commentary on autonomy economics (Werner, Schneider, Ryder)
- Tesla Semi ramp news; Waabi/Plus/Bot Auto/Torc progress
- Terminal openings, insurance/liability developments, driver-shortage data

## LOW / EXCLUDE (do not email)
- Daily price-action headlines ("AUR up/down X%")
- Syndicated PR with no new info, listicles, "best stocks to buy" content
- Retail-forum chatter (Reddit/StockTwits)
- Generic AV-industry think pieces with no new facts
