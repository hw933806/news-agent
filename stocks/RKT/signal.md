# RKT — Signal rules

Defines what counts as worth-an-email for Rocket. The ranker uses these to score and
order items highest → lowest within the RKT block. Tune freely; this is per-stock.

## HIGH signal (lead the section)
- Earnings, guidance changes, origination/servicing volume or margin (gain-on-sale) commentary
- Fed/FOMC decisions & guidance; big moves in the 30-yr mortgage rate or 10-yr Treasury
- M&A and integration milestones for Redfin / Mr. Cooper (synergies, write-downs, exec churn)
- Capital allocation: buybacks, dividends, MSR portfolio purchases/sales, debt actions
- Management changes (CEO/CFO/segment presidents), board/activist involvement
- Analyst rating changes & material price-target moves
- GSE/FHFA policy shifts (privatization, pricing, loan limits), CFPB/regulatory actions of size
- Direct competitor earnings/guidance that reset the read on origination (UWMC, PFSI)

## MEDIUM signal (include if room)
- MBA weekly mortgage-application swings (only if large or trend-breaking)
- Redfin/Zillow housing-market data with an origination read-through
- Competitor product/pricing moves (UWM broker incentives, SoFi/Better digital push)
- Servicing-portfolio deals elsewhere in the industry (marks the MSR book)
- Homebuilder rate-buydown/incentive commentary (demand read-through)

## LOW / EXCLUDE (do not email)
- Daily price-action headlines ("RKT up/down X%")
- Syndicated PR with no new info, listicles, "best stocks to buy" content
- Retail-forum chatter (Reddit/StockTwits)
- Minor personnel moves, routine marketing, sponsorships, Rocket Mortgage ad campaigns
